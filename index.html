Pollution Solution: Scientists Develop Living Plastics That Degrade in Compost or Erosion

https://scitechdaily.com/pollution-solution-scientists-develop-living-plastics-that-degrade-in-compost-or-erosion/ August 31, 2024

Plastics are widely used but difficult to degrade, posing an ecological challenge. A team from SIAT developed degradable “living plastics” using synthetic biology and polymer engineering. They engineered Bacillus subtilis spores to produce Burkholderia cepacia lipase (BC-lipase), an enzyme that breaks down plastic. These spores were mixed with poly(caprolactone) (PCL) to create the plastics, maintaining the material’s physical properties. When the plastic surface is eroded, the spores release the enzyme, leading to a nearly complete breakdown of the plastic.
